ANESTHESIOLOGIST TOOLKIT
.01 | DIFFICULT AIRWAY ALGORITHM
The Interactive Manual of Anesthesiology/ Anesthesiologist Toolkit is a collection of interactive apps to supplement, support and improve delivery of perioperative care in real time. 'Static' cognitive aids and checklists are a thing of the past. Interactive decision making tools and analytics are the future. Doctors should embrace technology -- their patients already have.

Apps are planned, designed and coded by anesthesiologist Stesha Doku, MD.
